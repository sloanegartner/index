About

I am currently double majoring in Psychology and Interactive Design at James Madison University. I am also an employee at Technology and Design, where I work as a photographer for University Unions. Being a Junior is exciting, but also nerve racking being so close to Senior year and graduating. One of the things I love about Madison is the pride everyone has from being a Duke and everyone holding doors. My favorite places at JMU are Carrier Library (cozy!), The Stadium, Quad, Madison Union, and Lakeside. Also at Madison, I play on the Club Field Hockey team and am currently the treasurer. Over the years I've played soccer, tennis, softball, track, and lacrosse before I landed on field hockey. It is one of my favorite pass times when I'm not in class or doing homework.

I was born in Washington D.C and grew up in Arlington VA. My parents met at JMU, my brother graduated from JMU, and my younger brother is currently a freshman here, so being a Duke runs in the family. My older brother, sister, and younger brother are studying Engineering and my younger sister is still in High School. The best part of going home is seeing me dogs!
